OUR STORY

A Mission Rooted in Family—

The name Legacy Concierge carries a promise — to honor your family's story with the same devotion we bring to our own.

Founded in 2022, Legacy was born from lived experience — from witnessing too many families navigate pain, uncertainty, and inadequate care when their loved ones needed compassion most.

We've felt that heartbreak. We've seen where the system falls short. And we built Legacy Concierge to be the answer. Our mission is simple yet sacred: to deliver elite, compassionate care that upholds dignity, trust, and humanity at every step. Every plan we design, every nurse we select, every partnership we form — all exist to ensure your loved one's journey is met with the reverence it deserves.

Because your family's legacy is more than a story — it's something worth protecting.

MEET JASON

Founder & CEO — Trust Through Origin

Jason Kidushim, Founder and CEO of Legacy Concierge, created this company with one mission: to redefine what care feels like.

His inspiration began with his grandfather — a decorated war hero whose battle with Alzheimer's revealed the deep flaws in our healthcare system. Witnessing that loss of dignity became the catalyst for Jason's life's work: to build a service where no family would ever have to endure that kind of neglect again.

After returning to California, Jason united his decades of leadership in healthcare operations, patient advocacy, and service design to establish Legacy Concierge — a model that blends clinical precision with human connection.

Under his guidance, Legacy has become more than a care provider — it's a movement dedicated to preserving dignity, honoring family, and setting a new standard for home healthcare.
